Malaysian Australian New Zealand Association (MANZA)

Friendship, community, and support since 1951

MANZA is a non-profit, volunteer-run organization bringing together the Australian, New Zealand, and international communities in Malaysia. Our mission is to foster friendship, cultural exchange, and mutual support through social events, charity work, and shared experiences.
